Perl mewn adfyd (1595) (PMA)

Huw Lewys. Perl mewn adfyd neu, Perl ysprydawl, gwyrthfawrocaf, yn dyscu i bôb dyn garu, a chofleidior groes... (Rhydychen: Joseph Barnes ai Printiodd, 1595), 1-101.

The text

The text is a translation of the third (1561) edition of Miles Coverdale's English translation of a treatise in German by the Swiss cleric Otto Werdmüller von Elg Ein Kleinot von Trost vnd Hilff in allerley Trübsalen (Zürich, 1548). Coverdale's English translation was published as A spyrytuall and moost precyouse pearle Teachyng all men to loue and imbrace the crosse (London, 1550).

Details of the encoding

Catchwords and marginal references have been omitted in the transcription. The text contains a number of hooked characters. <L> with a hook is represented as &lhook; and &Lhook; in the TEI-XML file, and as <l'> and <L'> in the display files. Hooked <d> is dealt with in similar fashion.

Further details

The text has previously been edited as Perl mewn adfyd gan Huw Lewys yn ôl argraffiad 1595 gyda rhagymadrodd gan W. J. Gruffydd (Caerdydd: Gwasg Prifysgol Cymru, 1929). This edition is based on the copy preserved in the National Library of Wales. The current transcription is based on the copy in the British Library.
