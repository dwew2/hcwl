The text

The corpus contains a selection of twenty-two ballads by Hugh Jones (Llangwm), published in the 1750s and 1760s.

The source

The ballads were transcribed from originals in the National Library of Wales: Cwpwrdd baledi 74, 76, 77, 83, 91, 95, 106, 108, 111, 112, 124, 171, 172, 477, 480, 483, 524, and 712. The first ballad of BWB 112 is also by Hugh Jones, but has been omitted.

Details of the encoding

Bibliographical details of the texts are given in bibl-elements within the sourceDesc-element of the TEI-header. Individual ballads are identified (in the id-attribute of the text-element accompanying the ballad itself, and in the sameAs-attribute of the bibl-element of the bibliographical details) using references derived from John Humphreys Davies's A bibliography of Welsh ballads (London: Honourable Society of Cymmrodorion, 1908-11). These take forms such as "BWB_327-2" where 327 refers to the number of the pamphlet in Davies's listing, and 2 indicates that this is the second ballad within that pamphlet.

Material external to the ballad (names of speakers, tunes, etc.) is not preceded by lb-elements and is not enclosed within l-elements, hence is not assigned a separate line number in any of the output files. Linebreaks within this material are marked with <lbNoN/> tags. The transcriptions generally try to preserve the line arrangements of the originals. However, inconsistencies of line arrangement, usually minor, within a single ballad have been removed. In the ballads from BWB 77 and 83(1), the lines have been extensively rearranged editorially, and the lines here do not correspond to those of the original; capitalisation there has been adjusted in order to reflect the new liniation, and double N-dashes marking rhymes have been removed. Indentation at the start of ballad verse has been removed where it occurs.

Where stanzas are numbered in the original, these numbers are marked up using the formword element <fw> ... </fw>.

Further details

BWB 95(2) and 124(2) have previously been edited by E. G. Millward in Blodeugerdd Barddas o gerddi rhydd y ddeunawfed ganrif (Llandybïe: Cyhoeddiadau Barddas, 1991), pp. 62-7.
