Texts

You can use these pages to do one of the following things:

  • view an individual text in a diplomatic edition (close to the original)
  • view an individual text in an edited form
  • download a text or texts to use for research purposes with some text-processing software (e.g. Concordance, WordSmith etc.)
  • download a text or texts in the form of the original XML-file
  • look for individual words within particular texts using a web concordance
  • look for individual words within the enture corpus using a web concordance

You can browse the texts by period, or by text type, or go straight to an alphabetical list of all texts.
