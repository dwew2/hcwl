Free metre poetry from Peniarth ms. 218 (1605-10)

  • Concordance to all the texts in Peniarth ms. 218

The selection contains the following items:

  1. ‘Ateb i Araith ddichan i’r gwragedd’ (pp. 58-73)

    This was previously edited in Canu rhydd cynnar, ed. T. H. Parry-Williams (Cardiff: Gwasg Prifysgol Cymru, 1932), 119-29.

  2. ‘Araith ddichan i’r gwragedd’ (pp. 79-95)

    This was previously edited in Canu rhydd cynnar, ed. T. H. Parry-Williams (Cardiff: Gwasg Prifysgol Cymru, 1932), 108-19.

  3. ‘Yr ymddiddan rhwng yr wttresswr a'r dyllvan’ (pp. 153-78)

    A previous edition, from NLW Add. 422 and (edition lines 306ff.) Peniarth ms. 218 can be found in Canu rhydd cynnar, ed. T. H. Parry-Williams (Cardiff: Gwasg Prifysgol Cymru, 1932), 436-46.

    • Diplomatic edition

  4. ‘Teithie Syr Sion Mandefyl’ (pp. 179-234)

    A previous edition, from NLW ms. 1553A, with variants from other manuscripts can be found in:

    W. Beynon Davies. 1931. ‘Siôn Mawndfil yn Gymraeg’. Bulletin of the Board of Celtic Studies 5: 287-327.

    The text there (NLW ms. 1553A) is slightly earlier (1604) than the text in Peniarth ms. 218 (finished 22 February 1610).

    • Diplomatic edition
