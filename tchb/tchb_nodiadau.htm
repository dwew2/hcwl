Corpws hanesyddol yr iaith Gymraeg: Tri Chryfion Byd (tchb)

Thomas Edwards (Twm o'r Nant), 1738-1810. Tri Chryfion Byd, sef Tlodi, Cariad, ac Angau. Yn y Canlyniad o hyn, y dangosir y modd y mae r Tri yn Gryfion byd. (dim lle / no place: dim cyhoeddwr / no publisher, [1789?]), testun cyflawn / entire text.

The quality of the typesetting of the original is poor, and the text is full of typesetting errors of a kind that suggest the typesetter knew Welsh either poorly or not at all. At one point there is an error in the pagination: page 48 is followed by another page numbered 48, before the pagination continues in the normal way (49, 50 etc.). These two pages are numbered 48a and 48b in the transcription files. The concordance files use line numbers only, hence are not affected by this.

The corpus editions generate a single series of line numbers for the entire text automatically. Stage directions, names of characters etc. are not assigned line numbers. The extensive corrections for typesetting errors are marked up in the usual way in the transcription files. A cast list is supplied.
