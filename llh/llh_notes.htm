Llyfr yr Homilïau (1606) (llh)

James, Edward. Pregethau a osodwyd allan trwy awdurdod i'w darllein ymhob Eglwys blwyf a phob capel er adailadaeth i'r bobl annyscedig. Gwedi eu troi i'r iaith Gymeraeg drwy waith Edward Iames. Robert Barker printiwr i odidawgaf fawrhydi y Brenin a'i Printiodd yn Llundain. Anno Dom. 1606. (Llundain: Robert Barker, 1606), i.153-76, ii.265-84, iii.165-84.

The text

The selection in the corpus consists of three parts, one from each volume:

  • from volume i., the chapters ‘Pregeth yn erbyn putteindra ac aflendid’;
  • from volume ii., ‘Pregeth yn yr hon y manegir y dylyid ministro gweddi gyhoeddus a’r Sacramentau mewn iaith a ddealler gan y gwrandawyr’;
  • from volume iii., ‘Pregeth am stât priodas’.

Details of the encoding

The original contains a number of erroneous page numbers. These are recorded in the n attribute of the <pb> element, in the form: corrected page number, underscore, T[ext], uncorrected page number. For instance <pb n="177_T175"> means that the original erroneously gives the page number as 175, but that this is page 177 in the sequence of page numbers.

The marginal notes of the original are omitted in the corpus transcription.

Further details

For further details, see also: Glanmor Williams, ‘Edward James a Llyfr yr Homilïau’, Morgannwg 25 (1981): 79-99.
