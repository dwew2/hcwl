Gweledigaetheu y Bardd Cwsc (gbc)

Wynne, Ellis. Gweledigaetheu y Bardd Cwsc (Llundain: E. Powell, 1703), 5-49, 54-77.

The text

The selection consists of parts i. (‘Gweledigaeth y Byd’) and ii. (‘Gweledigaeth Angeu yn ei Frenhinllys isa’). The poetry on pages 3-4 and 50-3 is omitted.

Details of the encoding

The text contains various pieces of poetry within in. These are marked up using the normal conventions for poetry. The poetry is not placed within a paragraph element.

Further details

There are several previous editions, including: Gweledigaetheu y Bardd Cwsc, ed. J. Morris Jones (Bangor: Jarvis & Foster, 1898).
